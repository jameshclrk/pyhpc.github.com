About

PyHPC events are a number of sessions at the Supercomputing Conference. These events typically include:

  • A tutorial on Python in the HPC space
  • A workshop highlighting the latest innovations with Python, and
  • A birds of a feather session of lightning talks discussing issues and new happenings
