Birds of a Feather Session

Tues, Nov 19, 12:15 - 13:15

The PyHPC Birds of a Feather session has been hosted at the Supercomputing conference for several years. This page is here to capture some of the outputs from the sessions.

Format

The PyHPC BoF takes the format of many lightning talks followed by a discussion on the Python ecosystem in HPC. Each lightning talk is roughly 5 minutes of time for a presenter to show off anything related to Python and HPC. Please email the organizers any slides you wish to show.
